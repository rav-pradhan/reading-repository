Impressions

Design systems provide a great deal of benefits for organisations with multiple digital teams that are looking to build services consistently and efficiently.

In Expressive Design Systems, Yesenia Perez-Cruz deftly assembles the various jigsaw pieces that lead to a purposeful and successful design system. She details the entire process from initial setup and technical implementation, all the way through to the importance of strong, open governance.
